Ye[a] (/jeɪ/ YAY; born Kanye Omari West /ˈkɑːnjeɪ/ KAHN-yay; June 8, 1977) is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Described as one of the greatest rappers of all time[4]
and one of the most prominent figures in hip-hop,[5] he is known for his varying musical style[6] and polarizing cultural and political commentary.[7][8]
